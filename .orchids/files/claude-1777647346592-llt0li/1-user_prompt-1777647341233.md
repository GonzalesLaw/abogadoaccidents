## 🚗 Car Accident Basics

### ❓ What should I do after a car accident in Southern California?

After a car accident in Southern California, call 911 if there are injuries, seek medical attention, document the scene with photos, and exchange information. Avoid giving recorded statements to insurance companies before speaking with a lawyer. Consulting Gonzales Law Offices can help protect your rights and maximize your claim.

---

### ❓ Do I need a car accident lawyer in California?

You are not legally required to hire a lawyer, but insurance companies often minimize payouts. A car accident lawyer can handle negotiations, prove liability, and pursue full compensation for medical bills, lost wages, and pain and suffering.

---

### ❓ How much is a car accident case worth in California?

Car accident settlements in California vary widely. Minor injury cases may settle for a few thousand dollars, while serious injuries involving long-term care can reach six or seven figures. Value depends on medical costs, liability, and insurance coverage.

---

### ❓ How long does a car accident settlement take in Southern California?

Most cases settle within a few months to a year. Complex cases involving serious injuries or disputed liability can take longer, especially if a lawsuit is filed.

### ❓ Who is at fault in a rear-end accident in California?

In most cases, the rear driver is presumed to be at fault for failing to maintain a safe following distance. However, exceptions exist depending on road conditions and driver behavior.

---

### ❓ How do insurance companies reduce payouts?

Insurance companies often minimize claims by disputing injuries, delaying responses, and offering low initial settlements. Legal representation helps counter these tactics.

### ❓ Should I accept the insurance company’s first offer?

No. Initial offers are often lower than what your case is worth. It’s best to review any offer with a lawyer before accepting.
