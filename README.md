## Frequently Asked Questions

### What should I do after a car accident in Fontana?
Call 911. Seek medical attention immediately — even if you feel fine. Document the scene with photos. Exchange information but do not admit fault. Do not give a recorded statement to any insurance company. Call Gonzales Law Offices 24/7 at **1-888-232-2898** for a free consultation.

### How much is my car accident case worth in California?
Settlement values range from thousands to millions depending on: injury severity, medical costs, lost wages, pain and suffering, liability clarity, and insurance coverage limits. Gonzales Law Offices has achieved individual settlements exceeding $4 million. Call for a free case evaluation.

### Do I need a lawyer for a minor car accident?
Yes — especially if injuries develop later. Whiplash and soft tissue injuries often worsen over days or weeks. Once you accept a settlement, you cannot reopen the claim. A free consultation costs you nothing.

### Who is at fault in a rear-end accident in California?
In most cases the rear driver is presumed at fault. Under California's pure comparative negligence law, you can still recover compensation even if partially at fault — reduced by your percentage of liability.

### Can I sue if the other driver has no insurance?
Yes. Your own uninsured/underinsured motorist (UM/UIM) coverage can compensate you. Gonzales Law Offices identifies every available source of recovery.

### How long do I have to file a car accident lawsuit in California?
2 years from the accident date for personal injury. 6 months for government entity claims (Caltrans, city, county). Do not wait — call immediately.

### How much does a car accident lawyer cost?
Nothing upfront. Gonzales Law Offices works on 100% contingency — fees come only from your settlement, and only if we win. If we don't win, you owe nothing.

### What is the Uber insurance coverage in a crash?
When a passenger is in the vehicle: $1 million liability. When the driver is logged in but no ride accepted: $50K/$100K. When the app is off: driver's personal insurance only.

### How much is a whiplash settlement in California?
$10,000 to $100,000+ depending on injury severity, treatment duration, and impact on life. Severe cervical disc injuries requiring surgery can reach six figures or more.

### What is a traumatic brain injury (TBI) worth in a lawsuit?
TBI cases in California regularly reach seven figures when the injury causes lasting impairment. Gonzales Law Offices works with neurologists and life-care planners to quantify the full lifetime value of your TBI case.

### How much is a truck accident settlement in California?
$500,000 to several million dollars. Commercial carriers carry $750K–$5M in liability insurance. Truck accident cases require immediate evidence preservation (black box, driver logs, dash cam footage).

### What is insurance bad faith in California?
When an insurer unreasonably denies, delays, or underpays a valid claim. You can sue for bad faith and recover punitive damages beyond policy limits. California has some of the strongest bad faith laws in the nation.

### How to File a Car Accident Claim in California
1. Report the accident to police and file CA DMV SR-1 if injury/damage > $1,000
2. Seek medical treatment immediately — same day if possible
3. Notify your insurance carrier (no recorded statements)
